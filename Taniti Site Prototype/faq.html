FAQs

WHAT YOU WANT TO KNOW ABOUT TANITI

About

  • What power plugs do I need?
    Taniti uses 120 volt power outlets, just like standard United States electrical fixtures.
  • What alcohol laws do I need to know?
    Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between the hours of midnight and 9:00AM.
  • What is the drinking age?
    Taniti's drinking age is 18, and the drinking age is not strictly enforced.
  • What language is spoken in Taniti?
    Taniti has a native language, but many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. Very little English is spoken in rural areas, especially by the older residents.
  • Is medical care available?
    There is one hospital and several major clinics on Taniti. The hospital has many multilingual employees.
  • Is there much crime on Taniti?
    Violent crime is very rare on Taniti, but as tourism has increased, so have instances of pickpocketing and other petty crimes. Taniti works very hard to reduce this crime, but remain vigilant and keep track of your belongings.
  • Are there local holidays? Will business be open on holidays?
    Taniti enjoys a number of national holidays, and many tourist attractions and restaurants will be closed on holidays, so visitors should plan accordingly.
  • What currency is used on Taniti?
    Taniti uses the US dollar as its currency, but many businesses will also accept euros and yen. Several banks facilitate currency exchange, and many businesses accept major credit cards.
